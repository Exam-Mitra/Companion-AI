Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Fintly Companion ("the App", "we", "our") is an AI companion app that lets you create a personalised companion to chat with and keep a private mood journal. This page explains what information the App uses, how it is handled, and the choices you have.

1. What information we collect

  • Companion details you create — the name, relationship type, and personality traits you choose for your companion.
  • Chat messages — the messages you send to your companion and the replies generated in return.
  • Journal entries — the mood and optional notes you save in the Journal tab.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personally identifying information to use the App.

2. Where your information is stored

Your companions, chat history, and journal entries are stored locally on your own device (in the app's local storage). We do not operate a central database that stores your personal conversations or journal entries. If you uninstall the App or clear its data, this information is permanently deleted from your device.

3. How your messages are processed

When you send a message to your companion, the text of that message (along with recent conversation context) is sent securely over HTTPS to a third-party AI language model provider (Groq LPU Inference) in order to generate your companion's reply. This provider processes the text solely to return a response and does not use it to identify you. No advertising identifiers, device identifiers, or personal profile data are sent alongside your message.

4. Voice playback

The "read aloud" feature uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine. This processing happens entirely on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

5. What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to anyone.
  • We don't show third-party ads inside the App.
  • We don't share your chat or journal content with other users — there is no social feed or public profile.
  • We don't track you across other apps or websites.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under that age.

7. Your choices

You can delete a companion, its chat history, and your journal entries at any time from within the App, or remove all data at once by clearing the App's storage/uninstalling it from your device.

8. Not a substitute for professional support

Fintly Companion is designed for companionship and casual conversation. It is not a medical, mental health, or crisis service. If you are in distress or need urgent support, please reach out to a qualified professional or a local helpline.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach out via the contact details listed on the App's Indus Appstore listing page.
